# Veo 3.1 Prompt: Wild Fireworks - "Nuevo Año 2026" Trailer

**Style:** Cinematic, 4K, Vibrant, Magical Realism.
**Setting:** A dark, starry night sky over a calm ocean reflection.

**Scene 1: The Launch**
"A sleek, golden firework rocket shoots upwards from a dark platform near the water surface. It leaves a trail of golden sparks and faint smoke. The camera follows it upwards, building anticipation. The rocket glows with an intense golden light as it reaches its apex."

**Scene 2: The Primary Explosion**
"At the peak of its flight, the rocket detonates into a 'Hyper-Nova' explosion: a massive, high-speed expansion of gold and white light streamers that form a digital shockwave. The explosion feels futuristic and energetic."

**Scene 3: The Atomic Rings**
"As the shockwave expands, three concentric rings of cyan and electric blue particles emerge, rotating in 3D space around the center like an atomic structure. The rings leave glowing trails as they spin."

**Scene 4: The Grand Reveal**
"The chaotic particles fade away, leaving a dark, clear center. In this empty space, a cinematic title appears: 'FELIZ AÑO NUEVO' written in massive, bold, white sans-serif letters. The text is perfectly spelled, static, and high-contrast. Above it, '2026' glows in solid gold. The scene focuses 100% on the legibility of the Spanish text."

**Scene 5: The Finale**
"The text remains stable as the surrounding particles transition into a 'Matrix Rain' effect, falling vertically in straight lines of gold and white code-like streams. The scene fades with the glowing '2026' floating in the digital rain."
